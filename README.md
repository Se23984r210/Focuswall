# Focuswall

Because your willpower is a joke. 

Focuswall is not a friendly productivity app. It is a hardcore, brutalist Chrome extension designed to force you to focus. It doesn't just block websites; it creates extreme psychological friction to stop you from procrastinating.

## Features

* **Absolute Lockdown:** Blocks distracting websites entirely, including embedded iframes.
* **Mathematical Friction:** Want to delete a website from your blocklist? You can't just click a button. You must solve a linear equation (e.g., 5x + 200 = -1000) to prove you're not just acting on impulse.
* **The Wall of Shame:** A built-in tracker that counts your daily failed attempts to bypass the lock and insults you based on your lack of discipline. Resets at midnight.
* **Anti-Cheat System:** Prevents you from disabling the extension during your locked hours by auto-closing the extensions page. If you uninstall it, it triggers a humiliating final search query.
* **Night Shift Proof:** Built-in scheduling logic that accurately tracks time even if your work session crosses midnight.



Since this extension is too aggressive for the standard Chrome Web Store, you have to install it manually:

1. Download or clone this repository to your computer.
2. Open Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Turn on **Developer mode** in the top right corner.
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the folder containing these files.
5. Open the extension, set your working hours, add your worst websites to the Blacklist, and try to survive.
